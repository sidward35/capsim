Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our team dynamic and what happens behind the scenes in our company

  • Can you describe the team dynamic?

    Right off the bat, we made sure to establish and maintain psychological safety. Each group member is expected to respect the views and opinions of others. While it's okay to disagree, it's important to remember we're all on the same team.

  • How did you decide on your companyâ€™s strategy?

    We had a tough time deciding if we should be broad, or focus on a niche market. Should we be cost leaders and undercut the market, or should we aim to be differentiators and not worry too much about competitive pricing? Ultimately, based on our previous experiences (namely the Capsim practice rounds), we decided that a broad cost leader strategy was something we were all interested in pursuing, and thought would be feasible given our overall comfort level with risk.

  • How did your group decide to delegate roles and get work done?

    Instead of assigning individual parts to each group member, which could lead to a lack of overall group input, and make it easy to play the blame game when it came poor decision-making, we decided that we should each pull up a different type of report/file and work together on the same decision segment.

  • What did your team do to motivate each other when work was stagnating?

    In order to bring up our motivation at points where it may have started to drop down a bit, we reminded ourselves that even though the market and industry overall are not in our control, we ultimately have complete control over our own decisions, and this made our decision-making process feel much more important to each of us.
